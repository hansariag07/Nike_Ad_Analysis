Storytelling is not just creativity—it’s strategy.

As part of our *Art of Storytelling* course in Business Administration, we analyzed Nike’s “You Can’t Stop Us” campaign as a powerful business story.

This project helped us understand:
• How narrative (characters, conflict, resolution) builds impact
• The role of emotional appeals in influencing audiences
• How brands use storytelling to create connection and trust

A great advertisement doesn’t just sell a product—it tells a story people feel part of.

Keytakeways- 
• Storytelling is the heart of marketing
• Emotions drive audience connection
• Strong narratives build powerful brands

This project changed how I look at advertisements—not just as content, but as meaningful business stories.
